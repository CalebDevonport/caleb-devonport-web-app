Game Reviews

Welcome to my game reviews!

I've played games my entire life, and so I've created this space to allow me to share my opinions with the world.

While I try to stay as objective as I can, the majority of these reviews will be positive - that's simply because I'm unlikely to commit a lot of time and effort to analyse a game enough for a review if I don't really like it.

I hope you enjoy reading some of my thoughts on a variety of different games. If you have any comments or want to discuss, then feel free to contact me through any of the options on the contact page.
